Foodcilitator

Foodcilitator is an automated service that identifies grocery products from photos. Text a photo of any grocery item to our number and receive an instant product identification reply.

Phone Number

How to Use

Send an MMS message with a photo of a grocery item to the number above. You will receive an automated SMS reply identifying the product.

SMS Consent & Disclosure

By texting a photo to +1 (833) 895-4370, you agree to receive automated SMS/MMS messages from Foodcilitator, including grocery product analysis and replies. This is a prototype service. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency depends on your usage. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

Privacy

Images you send are processed by the Claude AI API for product identification and are not stored by Foodcilitator. Standard carrier message logs may apply.
