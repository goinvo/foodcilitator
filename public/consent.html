Heard by GoInvo

Heard is a civic SMS service that connects people with the right representative for their concern. Text a description of a broken system or everyday inefficiency you've experienced, and Heard will identify which local, state, or federal official has jurisdiction — and provide a personalized script for calling their office.

Phone Number

+1 (833) 895-4370

How to Use

Text the number above to get started. Heard will ask for your mailing address to identify your representatives, then ask you to describe your concern. You'll receive back the relevant official's name, office phone number, and a ready-to-use call script.

SMS Consent & Disclosure

By texting +1 (833) 895-4370, you agree to receive automated SMS messages from GoInvo, including representative information and civic action scripts. This is a prototype service. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency depends on your usage. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

Privacy

Your messages are processed by the Claude AI API to identify the appropriate representative and generate a call script. Your address and messages are not stored beyond the active session. We do not share your phone number or message content with third parties other than your elected representatives' offices, and only when you choose to act.

Help & Support

Reply HELP to +1 (833) 895-4370 for assistance. Reply STOP to opt out and stop receiving messages at any time.
